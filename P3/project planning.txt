create 4 topics:

	- DBInfo (list of weather stations and locations)
	- Results (calculate metrics and store them in the database)
	- Standard Weather (location and temperature)
	- Weather Alerts (type of alert, location and name)


file.java -> connect to dbms, get info from dbms and send to topic (producer)

other.java -> receive info from dbinfo and process it (random temperature) and send it to Standard Weather topic
	   -> receive info from dbinfo and process it (random alert and name) and send it to Weather Alert topic

metrics.java -> receive info from 2 topics, calculate metrics and then store it in the dbms
